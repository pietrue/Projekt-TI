Genres worth checking out

Here you can find the up and coming alternative genres that

Neo-Shoegaze

This genre redefines what modern band music is. Finding its footing in the early 00's the internet played a huge role in its comeup. Music-heads around the world started sharing their music taste on websites like 4Chan and Reddit serving as the breeding ground for the shoegaze revival era. Many bands started forming soon after, mixing and matching styles and themes forming what we know now as Neo-Shoegaze.

progressive Alt Rock

As a movement it presents new horizons for the alt rock genre, with complex chord progressions and atypical scale patterns for vocals and instrumental melodies. Coming on the heels of 90s alt rock era the leading bands got bored of the samey style presented the decade before coming up with experimental yet pleasant to the ear ideas and solutions, creating the "format" of the subgenre.

Zoomergaze

Coming from internet culture and the diversity of artists streaming services presented, Zoomergaze projects focus on mixing overblown well produced shoegaze melodies and chords with grungy and modern techniques which Digital audio workspaces offer for producers. This subgenre of shoegaze is still finding its footing in the modern music scene and no doubt will become one of the most popular and populated genres of our era.
